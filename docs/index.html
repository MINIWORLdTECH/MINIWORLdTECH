Frequently Asked Questions.

How to apply for internships? ▼

To apply for the upcoming batch of internships, you must fill out the interest form in this domain. After that, you will receive the selection certificate from us if selected.

How to fill the interest form? ▼

You will get the interest form for all the internships domain in above mentioned internship column. Please click on apply now button. Your response will help us to understand your current skills and need for training during the internship, if any.

I've completed all the necessary steps, whats next? ▼

If you have filled all the fields in the interest form correctly, you will receive a selection certificate from us before your internship starts. All important information and announcements related to the internship are communicated through the discussion from the Miniworld network on LinkedIn.

What will be the duration of the internships? ▼

In every domain, the duration of the internship is 4 weeks.

I have completed my tasks well before deadline. When will I get the certificate? ▼

You can submit your task through the task submission form if you have received it or wait for it to be shared. If you have submitted the task before the deadline, please expect your completion certificate in the 1st week of next month.
